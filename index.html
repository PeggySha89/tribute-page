Biography

Rihanna is a wonderful and fabulous talented artist. She was born in Barbados on February 20, 1988. She has so many talents, ranging from singer to writer to actress to businesswoman. She won so many awards for her work. She came into fame with her single "Pon de Replay" in 2005. From there, her stardom kept increasing. In 2018, she launched her own lingery line, Savage X Fenty. This business in addition to her music made her the youngest self-made female billionaire in 2022. In June 2023, Rihanna stepped down as CEO of Savage X Fenty. She recently became a mother of two with partner A$AP Rocky.
